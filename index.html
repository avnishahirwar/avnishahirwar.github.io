My name is Avnish Ahirwar. I am an author, working professional, and web developer with a passion for innovation, technology, and continuous learning. I am the author of the book Flight 143: Khaufnak Pal, reflecting my interest in storytelling and creative writing. Alongside my professional career, I enjoy designing modern websites and exploring digital solutions that combine creativity with functionality. Currently, I am based in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, and serve at the Chief Minister's Office, Government of Madhya Pradesh. My primary responsibility is managing the online registration and application processing under the Chief Minister's Voluntary Grant Scheme, ensuring efficient and accurate service delivery. Beyond my professional life, I have a strong passion for traveling, discovering new destinations, and experiencing royal heritage, luxury lifestyles, and premium architecture. I believe that exploring different cultures and environments broadens perspective and inspires growth. My long-term vision is to become a successful entrepreneur by building my own company into a high-growth enterprise with a multi-crore turnover. I am committed to continuous self-improvement, leadership, innovation, and creating meaningful value through business and technology.
